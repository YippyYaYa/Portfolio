TokTokTok was founded in 2020 as the father and son decided to open up a workshop to service their long-time customers. Driven by a passion to inherit his father's craft, Alan, the co-founder of the company, started his apprenticeship at age 19. With over 50 Years of craftsmanship combined, they strive to achieve one goal: to craft beautiful, durable and timeless furniture for people.
